Laiva is a decentralized, privacy-focused messenger that transforms your standard email server into a secure chat platform. While industry giants collect your metadata, Laiva simply sends encrypted emails that no one but you and your recipient can read.

Key Features:

- **True Independence:** Use any email provider (IMAP/SMTP) or host your own server. No central authority, no proprietary servers.
- **End-to-End Encryption (E2EE):** Messages are encrypted locally on your device using AES + RSA. Your provider and third parties cannot access your conversations.
- **No Data Collection:** No phone number required, no contact syncing, and no tracking.
- **Open Source:** Full transparency — the code is open for audit and contributions.

Technical Stack:
- UI: Jetpack Compose with Material 3.
- Database: Room (SQLite) - all data stays on your device.
- Network: JavaMail API.

Note: This is an early alpha version. As a student project, it may contain bugs in audio playback and message status indicators. We appreciate your feedback and contributions!
